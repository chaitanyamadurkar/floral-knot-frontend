Refund & Cancellation Policy

We value your trust and strive to provide high-quality products. Please read our refund and cancellation policy carefully.

Refund Eligibility 🔄️

Refunds are applicable under the following conditions:

  • Product received is damaged or defective
  • Wrong product delivered
  • Missing or incomplete items

▶️ Uncut parcel opening video is mandatory for any damage claims.(Claims without video will not be considered)

Non-Refundable Cases 🚫

  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Damage caused by misuse or mishandling
  • Products purchased during sales or discounts

Refund Process 🕒

  1. Contact us via email with your order ID and issue details
  2. Our team will review your request
  3. If approved, refund will be processed within 5–7 business days
  4. Refund will be credited to the original payment method

Cancellation Policy

Orders can be cancelled only before shipment. Once the product has been shipped, cancellation requests will not be accepted.
